Adamson Portal


Students will be able to access all of the online services that are offered by Adamson University via a website known as the Adamson Portal. Some of these online services include Adamson E-learning, the Finance Portal, the Adamson Brand Book, and the Health Check List. Another learners management system offered by Adamson University, the Adamson E-learning, may also be found through this site. Adamson University is the provider of this system The latest school updates and announcements may be found on this portal.

OPAC


The Online Public Access Catalogue (also known as OPAC) is a web-based tool that Adamson University offers to its students, faculty, and staff in order to facilitate remote access to the library's contents. This online collection offers digital versions of thousands of books and articles for study purposes. The system has features that make it simple to search for and filter available materials.
