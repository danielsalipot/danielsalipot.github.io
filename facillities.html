Computer Laboratories

The Adamson University boast multiple Computer laboratories all scattered across the campus. These Computers are equipped with different softwares that will help in the studies of students from different courses. These computer laboratories are equipped with televisions to for better viewing the presentation of the professorrs.

Mac Laboratories

Computer Laboratories are available to students at Adamson University, and the university's IMac computers are of the highest quality. These laptops come preloaded with a variety of software programs that will assist students in their studies in a variety of subject areas.

Classrooms

The classrooms at Adamson Universities are outfitted with a variety of technologies such as televisions, projectors, cameras, and air purifiers in order to enhance the educational experience for students and to maintain a learning environment that is conducive to their physical and mental well-being.

Libraries

There are a number of libraries located throughout the several campuses that make up Adamson University. The patrons of these libraries have access to computers, which are provided for their convenience. Additionally, OPAC terminals are available for use in the Library. Students may use these terminals to browse the online catalogue that is made available by the institution.
